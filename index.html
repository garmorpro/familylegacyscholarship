Mission

The Morgan Legacy Scholarship was established by the Morgan Family to honor our family’s legacy by supporting graduating seniors of Battery Creek High School who demonstrate strong character, perseverance, and a commitment to building a better future.

Scholarship Overview

Award Amount

$1000 awarded to one graduating senior

Eligibility

Open to graduating seniors of Battery Creek High School
